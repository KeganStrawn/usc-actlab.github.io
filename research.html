Mixed Reality for Robotics

We show-case Mixed Reality as a valuable tool for research and development in robotics. Specifically, we address how to reduce the gap between simulation and implementation and safety concerns with human-robot interaction.

We investigate possible approaches to modelling human group behavior and coordination when solving collective tasks. We conduct experiments on groups of humans and study the group behaviors that emerge.
